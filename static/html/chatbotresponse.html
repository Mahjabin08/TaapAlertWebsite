How can I protect my crops during a heatwave?
To protect your crops during a heatwave, consider these strategies:
Mulching: Apply a thick layer of organic mulch around your plants to retain soil moisture
and regulate temperature.
Irrigation: Water your crops early in the morning or late in the evening to minimize evaporation
 Drip irrigation systems work best.
Shade: Use shade cloths or netting to shield delicate plants from direct sunlight.
Drought-Resistant Crops: If heatwaves are frequent, consider planting drought-tolerant
 varieties that can withstand high temperatures.
Remember to monitor your soil moisture and adjust your watering schedule as needed!
